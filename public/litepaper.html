HedraFi Litepaper

The Sovereign Infrastructure of Hedera

1. Executive Summary

HedraFi is a next-generation DeFi + creator ecosystem built natively on the Hedera Mainnet.

Our Vision: For too long, the blockchain space has been split into two extremes: enterprise infrastructure that feels too distant from retail adoption, and retail ecosystems filled with hype but lacking long-term stability.

HedraFi exists to bridge that gap by building a system that brings real utility, creator activity, and community participation into Hedera—without sacrificing security or sustainability.

Our first phase begins with staking and community onboarding, followed by a creator-powered marketplace, and then long-term expansion into Real-World Assets (RWA).

HedraFi is currently supported through the Thrive Hedera program, aligning our vision with the most stable enterprise-grade network in Web3.

2. The Heart of the Protocol: Why We Stake

We didn’t launch staking to take from the community. We launched it to reward early believers and build a foundation of real participation.

  • Community-First Distribution: Staking is the primary mechanism used to distribute 150M $HRT to early supporters.
  • Non-Custodial by Design: HedraFi staking is fully non-custodial. The team does not hold user funds and cannot access user HBAR.
  • Proof of Conviction: Instead of relying on hollow hype, staking creates measurable proof that HedraFi has a growing base of committed users supporting the ecosystem. Users can stake as little as 10 HBAR to earn $HRT rewards, with no lockup or withdrawal restrictions. This ensures that staking is a true expression of user conviction, not a forced commitment.

3. The Pioneer Council: The First 400

Governance shouldn’t be driven by short-term speculation. It should be shaped by committed early builders and supporters.

  • High-Conviction Governance: The council exists to support structured decision-making and community direction as HedraFi scales.
  • Council Recognition: Council members receive a Premium Pioneer Council NFT Badge and permanent recognition as part of the foundation layer of HedraFi’s growth. This exclusive digital asset—available to the first 400 unique addresses to participate before May 7, 2026—grants holders long-term governance influence within the HedraFi's framework.

4. $HRT Token Utility

$HRT is HedraFi’s native utility token. It is designed to power marketplace activity, ecosystem incentives, and long-term protocol governance.

  • Marketplace Visibility & Boosting: Creators and collectors will be able to use $HRT to boost collections, secure premium visibility, and increase marketplace discovery.
  • Exclusive Access & Early Drops: Certain marketplace drops, premium tools, and future ecosystem opportunities will be gated through $HRT-based access.
  • Tiered Rewards System: Users who stake $HRT alongside HBAR will unlock higher reward tiers and additional protocol incentives over time.
  • Governance Influence: Beyond the Pioneer Council, $HRT will serve as governance weight for broader protocol decisions, including marketplace parameters, incentive distribution, and ecosystem integrations.

$HRT distribution is handled automatically by Hedera Mainnet smart contracts for transparency and verifiability.

We didn't launch our staking program to take from the community; we launched it to give.

  • Community-First Distribution: Staking is the engine we use to distribute 150M $HRT tokens to our earliest believers.
  • The "Win-Win" Model: Our staking is 100% non-custodial. We never take possession of your HBAR. Your funds stay under your control at all times—you lose nothing, yet you earn a share in the future of the ecosystem.
  • Proving Conviction: Instead of "hollow hype," we use staking to prove to the ecosystem that HedraFi has a foundation of strong believers. Our aim is to give to the community, not take from them.

4. $HRT Token Utility

$HRT is the fuel powering the Marketplace and the Institutional Bridge for Real-World Assets (RWA). It is designed to be a high-velocity utility token that rewards long-term believers with exclusive protocol advantages.

  • Premium Featuring & Visibility: Creators and collectors can use $HRT to "Boost" their assets on the marketplace, securing front-page placement and premium highlighting to increase visibility and sales velocity.
  • Exclusive Access & Early-Bird Drops: Certain high-tier NFT collections and "Institutional Bridge" asset pools will be gated, requiring $HRT to unlock access before they open to the general public.
  • The Reward Multiplier: Staking $HRT alongside HBAR unlocks "Tiered Rewards," allowing dedicated users to accelerate their yield and earn higher percentages of the daily distribution.

$HRT distribution is handled 100% automatically by Mainnet smart contracts, ensuring instant utility and transparency.
